✦ The Person Behind the Work

About Me

Tolu Adejuwon is a Senior FX Analyst at a Big Five Canadian Bank with 7+ years of institutional financial operations experience. The work sits at the intersection of high-accuracy execution and process infrastructure: multi-currency FX reconciliation, Visa dispute management, fraud risk, and the operational design that allows complex financial systems to scale reliably.

The career began in Lagos, covering construction supervision, product distribution reconciliation for Nigerian Breweries, government payment administration during national service, and a full vehicle sales cycle at a Ford dealership. Each role reinforced the same principle: diagnose the actual constraint before proposing any solution.

That discipline carried into four progressive roles at a Big Five Canadian Bank: Chargeback Analyst, Business Systems Analyst, Fraud Analyst, and Senior FX Analyst. Each role built directly on the one before. The result is a practitioner who does not simply operate financial systems, but identifies where they break and rebuilds them to run without ongoing intervention.

Credentials include a Certified Scrum Master (CSM) certification and post-graduate certificates in IT Project Management (Humber College) and International Business Management (Georgian College, co-op completed). Project delivery methodology has been applied consistently across both institutional banking operations and independent client engagements.

Outside of professional work: follows AI and IoT as applied tools rather than industry trends, approaches game design as a lens for systems thinking, and is committed to teaching as a means of compounding the value of what has been learned.
